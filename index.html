Event rules

We are the union of bike shops all around Dublin, we try to popularize the cycling culture by setting up events. Cycling is one of the most ecological ways to move around the city, it significantly reduces the release of CO2 gas into the atmosphere comparing to any type of car even if it’s electric.

What are the profits for you?

Being a member of the cycling community means having a lot of profits like free bike servicing and having privileged access to our events, don’t forget about the prizes such as grants or trips which you can receive by winning our competitions. Also, you will be able to take part in the organization of our events.

Becoming a member

To become a member, you can fill in the membership form, then you will be contacted by our admins to submit your application. Then you will receive a membership card which will allow you to get discounts in shops and participate in our events.

Event rules

  • Participants must use a bicycle in good working condition.
  • Helmets are mandatory and must comply with safety standards.
  • Obey the official start signal.
  • Respect traffic rules and signals if applicable.
  • one ticket-one person
  • Riders must follow the designated course and not take shortcuts.
  • Maintain a safe distance from the rider in front, if drafting is not allowed.
  • Adherence to anti-doping rules and regulations is mandatory.
  • Follow rules regarding feeding zones.
  • Respect the environment and follow any rules related to environmental protection.
