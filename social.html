What events do we host?

Events Bike & Dub Audaxes

We are the union of bike shops all around Dublin, we try to popularize the cycling culture by setting up events. Every member can host an event at any time, we also have annual events which are hosted by the heads of the community.

Bike & Dub

Every year, Dublin comes alive with the vibrant energy of the annual cycling event that captivates both enthusiasts and spectators alike. The city's streets transform into a colorful tapestry of cyclists, pedaling through iconic landmarks and scenic routes.

Audaxes

With routes ranging from 200 to 600 kilometers, our non-competitive event emphasizes endurance, self-sufficiency, and the joy of long-distance cycling. Inclusivity is key, welcoming riders of all levels to explore Ireland's scenic landscapes while forging connections with fellow cyclists.
